RATING SCALE: (Based on Patricia Benner's Novice to Expert Theory)

Expert A nurse who has a deep connection and understanding of the situation, no longer relies on analytic principle; instead the expert has an intuitive grasp of situations that is utilized to determine actions 95-100
Proficient A nurse who has a holistic understanding of the patient, thus having the power of decision making 85-95
Competent A nurse who starts to plan his or her actions in terms of long-range goals based on considerable conscious, abstract, analytic contemplation of the problem 75-85
Advanced Beginner A nurse who can demonstrate marginally acceptable performance, one who have coped with enough real situations 60-75
Novice A beginner with no experience of the situation in which they are expected to perform. <60
